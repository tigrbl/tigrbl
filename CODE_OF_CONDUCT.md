# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a professional, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to:

- act professionally and respectfully
- give and receive technical feedback constructively
- assume good faith while remaining precise about engineering concerns
- avoid personal attacks, insults, or harassment
- respect differing experience levels and communication styles

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- abusive language or personal attacks
- deliberate disruption of collaboration spaces
- publishing private information without permission
- retaliation against reporters or maintainers

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, review threads, and any project spaces used for collaboration.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers through the repository hosting platform’s private contact or security/advisory channel. If a dedicated maintainer contact is added later, `SECURITY.md` and `README.md` should be updated to point to it.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and its contributors.
